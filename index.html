Get to know me!

Hi! I'm Kevin. Thanks for taking the time to check out my portfolio. Currently I'm a senior at ASU where I've been studying Human Systems Engineering, with a UX concentration. My background spans across user research, interaction design, and frontend development. I really care about the full picture, from understanding why users struggle to building something that actually works better.

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." - Steve Jobs

Before focusing on design, I spent four years at the Arizona Center for Nature Conservation managing 100+ staff at the Phoenix Zoo. Reading people quickly, communicating under pressure, caring about the experience. All those years I was doing UX thinking long before I had a name for it.

Outside of work, I've been coaching youth flag football and basketball for years; hiking trails all over Arizona; travelling and seeing new locations, cultures, and meeting locals; watching my son grow up (way too fast!); working through my seemingly neverending backlog of games; and loving my cats.
