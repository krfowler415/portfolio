A bit about me

I'm a senior at ASU studying Human Systems Engineering with a UX concentration. My background spans user research, interaction design, and frontend development — I care about the full picture, from understanding why users struggle to building something that actually works better.

"There's a softness and a hardness to the things worth understanding — people, nature, good design."

Before focusing on design, I spent four years at the Arizona Center for Nature Conservation managing 100+ staff at the Phoenix Zoo. Reading people quickly, communicating under pressure, caring about the experience — I was doing UX thinking long before I had a name for it.

Outside of work: coaching youth flag football and basketball in Chandler, hiking Arizona trails, watching my son grow up, and two cats who largely run the household.
